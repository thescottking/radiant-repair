About Us

Solar should be effortless for the people who own it.

Based in Marfa, Texas, Radiant Repair is a full-service solar electricity management and service organization. We help homeowners and businesses get the most from their solar investment — from system health and performance monitoring to on-site maintenance, repairs, and upgrades. We take on the complexity of managing and optimizing your system, so you can enjoy reliable, efficient clean energy without the headaches.
